Family

Family brings us up and protects us since we were just a newly born baby until we become an individual. Family gives us comfort, sympathy, faith, and hope. However, nowadays, because of life pressure and the huge amount of work we have to deal with, most parents are ‚Äúaddicted‚Äù to making money and forget that they still need to take care of their family. Moreover, there are so many abandoned, homeless children out there. Those children also desire a life in which they have a family and parents to take care of them every day. The immense lack of emotion leads to huge changes in children's personality development. The changes could affect the child's future, limit the growth of the children and they can even become a depersonalized person.

Besides hoping to solve those problems which we mentioned before, we made this website with the inspiration of a Japanese animation about family. The animation talks about a family who was living a happy life and there were a father, a mother, and a lovely daughter. But unfortunately, the mother passed away because of cancer. Then, the father was too grievous that he left his child and whenever he saw his daughter, he felt sorrowful because her face looked the same as his late wife - who he loved the most. A few years later, whereas the child was living with her grandparents, the father came back and wanted to take care of her. Day by day, they gradually became a ‚Äú real family‚Äù despite the loneliness, emptiness throughout a long time. They tried to understand each other, tried to heal each other, and fill up the blank that the mother had left. Therefore, we can see the incredibly important role of family. And that story has made us extremely want to design this website ‚ÄúSense of Belonging", in order to help everyone realize how precious and valuable our families are.
